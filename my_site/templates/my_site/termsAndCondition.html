2. Eligibility

- Self-drive rentals are only available in Kumasi.
- You must be at least 35 years old and possess a valid Ghanaian driver’s license for more than one year to rent a self-drive vehicle.
- A valid visa passport is required for self-drive rentals.
- For all rentals, a Ghana Card, digital address, and an emergency contact are required.
- A guarantor is required for the driver’s contract in self-drive rentals.

3. Booking and Payment

- Reservations can be made through our office or customer service team.
- Self-drive rentals require full payment upfront, including a cash lock ranging from GHC 2000 to GHC 5000, depending on the car chosen.
- Daily Charge: Ranges from GHC 500 to GHC 5000, depending on the car chosen.
- Payments can be made via Cash, Mobile Money (MoMo), or Bank Transfer.
- Customers will receive daily mileage updates via WhatsApp.

- Vehicles must not be used for illegal activities, sub-leased, or used for unauthorized commercial purposes.

5. Cancellations and Refunds

- Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the rental start time will receive a full refund.
- Late cancellations (on the same day) will be charged one full day’s rental fee.
- Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.

6. ⁠Late Return Policy

- Late vehicle returns will result in an additional daily rental charge.
- Any outstanding charges will be deducted from the cash lock.

7. Fuel Policy
