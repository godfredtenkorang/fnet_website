Pay and Drive: Daily charge: GHC ____________________________ for ____________________________ km in ____________________________. Any additional kilometers will be charged at GHC 3 per kilometer. Driver accommodation fee: GHC 200 per day for rentals longer than 1 day.

4. Mileage Policy: The mileage charge is GHC 3 per kilometer for extra kilometers assigned to be used beyond the initial ____________________________ km. Mileage used will be sent to your WhatsApp line at the end of each day.

5. Cash Lock Return (Self-Drive Only): After the trip, if the vehicle is returned in the same condition, the GHC ____________________________ cash lock will be returned to the customer. If there are any damages or issues, the repair costs will be deducted from the cash lock. If the repair cost exceeds the cash lock amount, the customer will be liable for the difference.

6. Vehicle Condition: The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as it was provided. Any damages or excessive wear and tear will be charged to the customer.

7. Insurance: The vehicle is insured, but the customer is liable for any deductible or damages not covered by insurance if it is on self-drive.

8. Use of Vehicle: The vehicle must be driven solely by the customer or a pre-authorized driver (for Self-Drive). The vehicle must not be used for illegal activities, towing, or carrying hazardous materials.

9. Termination of Contract: The customer can terminate the contract up to 24 hours before the scheduled start time with no penalty. If the contract is terminated less than 24 hours before the trip, the customer will lose 40% of the daily charge.

10. Refund Policy: No refunds will be given for early returns or unused kilometers. Any remaining fuel balance will be refunded after the tank is refilled via cash or MoMo account.

11. Dispute Resolution: Any disputes arising from this contract will be resolved through mutual negotiation. If necessary, disputes will be settled in accordance with the laws of Ghana.
